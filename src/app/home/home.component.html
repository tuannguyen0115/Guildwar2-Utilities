Guild War 2 Utilities

This is a collection of various utilities,intended to make the daily farm easier to manage.

TP Profit Calculation

Tool used to calculate the profit/lose of a transaction after adjusting to the mats cost, list and selling fee.

Istan Shipment Calculation

Tool used to calculate the profit/lose of a shipment in Domain of Istan, adjusting to the shipment cost, list and selling fee.
